Sports & Activities

Weekly games & special events on Independence & Republic Day

Cricket

Students play cricket every weekend to improve teamwork, coordination, and sportsmanship.

Kabaddi

Exciting kabaddi matches are conducted on weekends, promoting physical fitness and strategic thinking.

Kho-Kho

Kho-Kho sessions are held regularly to enhance agility, speed, and teamwork among students.

Running Races

Students participate in running races every weekend to build stamina and encourage healthy competition.

Lemon & Spoon Race

Fun-filled lemon & spoon races are organized to promote balance, focus, and enjoyment.

Tug of War

Tug of War matches are conducted every weekend and during special events on 15th August and 26th January.
